# Bayesian Neural Network Based Intelligent Tire Force Estimation

## One-line thesis

Tire force estimation should expose uncertainty, especially under high slip, low friction, or distribution shift.

## Problem / Gap

Deterministic tire force estimates hide whether the force prediction is reliable.

## Method

Bayesian neural network tire force estimation.

## Key Results

First project version should use simpler ensemble uncertainty, but tire-level uncertainty remains important.

## Assumptions

Uncertainty can be calibrated from data.

## Limitations / Failure Modes

BNN implementation and calibration are more complex than ensembles.

## Reusable Ingredients
