# Spatio-Temporal Tire-Road Friction Estimation for Automated Vehicles

## One-line thesis

Road friction should be modeled as a temporal or spatial-temporal latent process rather than independent per-frame classification.

## Problem / Gap

Road transitions create delayed, history-dependent vehicle responses.

## Method

Spatio-temporal friction estimation for automated vehicles.

## Key Results

Use history encoder and smooth latent μ dynamics, with transition-aware relaxation.

## Assumptions

Temporal history contains friction information.

## Limitations / Failure Modes

May require vision or map priors for anticipatory friction.

## Reusable Ingredients

Latent friction dynamics; transition smoothing.

## Open Questions
