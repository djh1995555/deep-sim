# Learning and Control of Safety-Critical Systems: A Gaussian Process Based Approach

## One-line thesis

Gaussian-process dynamics learning is valuable for calibrated uncertainty and safety constraints, but may be too heavy for the first engineering model.

## Problem / Gap

Data-driven dynamics need uncertainty estimates when used in safety-critical systems.

## Method

Survey/tutorial on GP-based learning and control.

## Key Results

Supports using epistemic uncertainty and conservative prediction under model mismatch.

## Assumptions

GP assumptions and computational budget are acceptable.

## Limitations / Failure Modes

High-dimensional, large-scale rollout can be expensive.

## Reusable Ingredients
