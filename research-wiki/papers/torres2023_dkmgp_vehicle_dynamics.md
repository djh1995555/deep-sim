# Deep Kernelized and Multilayer Gaussian Processes for Autonomous Vehicle Dynamics Identification

## One-line thesis

Deep kernel and multilayer Gaussian processes provide uncertainty-aware nonlinear dynamics identification.

## Problem / Gap

Vehicle dynamics are nonlinear and uncertain, especially outside nominal operating regimes.

## Method

Deep kernelized and multilayer Gaussian processes for autonomous vehicle dynamics identification.

## Key Results

Useful as an uncertainty-aware modeling reference, but heavier than deep ensembles for first implementation.

## Assumptions

Sufficient data and GP-style inference are available.

## Limitations / Failure Modes
