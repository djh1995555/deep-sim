# Estimation of Wheel-Ground Contact Normal Forces of a Vehicle from Experimental Data Validation

## One-line thesis

Four-wheel normal load estimation requires validation because transient load transfer strongly affects tire force capacity.

## Problem / Gap

Analytical normal load models may miss transient and suspension effects.

## Method

Estimate wheel-ground contact normal forces and validate from experimental data.

## Key Results

Use high-fidelity simulator true `Fz_i` as teacher signal and evaluate normal-load prediction separately.

## Assumptions

Experimental or simulated reference normal forces are available for validation.

## Limitations / Failure Modes

Real vehicles may not provide direct normal force measurements.

## Reusable Ingredients

`Fz_i` validation metrics; transient load transfer checks.
